New to Basel as a student

Before:

Secure your accommodation.

After:

  1. Sign the accommodation contract.
  2. Have your name on the post box.
  3. Buy a simcard with your passport.
  4. Visit the canton office.
  5. Open a bank account.
  6. Register your postal address.
  7. Compulsory health insurance.
